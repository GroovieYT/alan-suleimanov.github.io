Our Story

Irmin Tech is a second-generation renewable feedstock supplier focused on intermediate crops for advanced biofuels. We bridge agriculture and renewable fuels markets with supply programs designed to be practical—contractable, routable, and documentation-ready.

We are based in Germany and operate across Bucharest (Romania) and Kazakhstan. Our work spans origin build-out, partner coordination, and buyer-facing interfaces—ensuring the chain from field to refinery gate is aligned on quality, routing, and traceability expectations.

Mission

Build reliable intermediate-crop feedstock supply for SAF and renewable fuels value chains—through disciplined execution and traceability-ready reporting.

What makes Irmin Tech different

  • Intermediate-crop focus: Camelina sativa and Carinata programs designed around realistic grower adoption and routing.
  • Traceability-ready execution: chain-of-custody thinking built into contracting, batching, and handoffs.
  • Partnership model: structured interfaces with growers, aggregators, logistics, and crushing partners.
  • B2B reliability: clear specs, predictable timelines, and transparent assumptions—no hype.

Sustainability & compliance readiness

Our programs are designed to align with RED III expectations in documentation and reporting practices, based on what downstream buyers typically require to support regulatory and voluntary scheme needs.

We operate with a certification-ready mindset for schemes such as ISCC / CORSIA where applicable—meaning the supply chain is structured to support audit-ready data capture and verification, subject to partner scope and project requirements.
